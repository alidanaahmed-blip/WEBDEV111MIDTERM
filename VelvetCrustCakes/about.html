Our kitchen, your celebration

Velvet Crust Cakes is a small team of pastry stylists crafting cloud-soft layers, seasonal compotes, and modern finishes. We bake to order, never freeze, and keep batches small so every slice tastes like it was made just for you.

Who we are

Founded in 2018 by pastry chef Mara Velasquez, our kitchen started as a weekend pop-up for friends. Today we are a two-oven studio focused on custom celebration cakes and petite dessert bars. We serve the San Francisco Bay Area with local pickups and limited deliveries.

Our style blends classic French techniques with playful, modern styling: sharp edges, airy mousse fillings, and fruit-forward colors that photograph beautifully without artificial dyes.

How we bake

Single-day batches
Sponges and fillings are mixed the morning of your pickup window.
Real fruit color
Strawberry, raspberry, citrus, and matcha give the hue; no synthetic dyes.
Balanced sweetness
Swiss meringue buttercream keeps every bite light, not cloying.
Allergy-aware
We offer nut-free builds on request; our kitchen handles tree nuts.
